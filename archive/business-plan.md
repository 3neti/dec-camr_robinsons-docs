# Business Plan

## Product Summary
Text Commander (txtxcmdr) is a turnkey SMS broadcast platform bundled with a high-value sender ID ("Quezon City"). This makes it ideal for LGUs, public information offices, and political campaigns.

## Revenue Model
- One-time sale of sender ID + platform + infra
- Optional: annual support/upgrade retainer

## Buyers
- LGU offices (PIO, DRRM, health)
- Political candidates (esp. city-wide races)
- NGOs or local civic groups

## Competitive Edge
- You own the sender ID.
- You sell a working product, not just software.
